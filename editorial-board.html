Editorial Board

KJMR maintains a committed editorial team to ensure integrity, transparency, and scholarly quality in publishing. Manuscripts are handled through a double-blind peer review process and evaluated for originality, rigor, clarity, ethical compliance, and contribution to multidisciplinary scholarship.
