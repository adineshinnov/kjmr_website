Submission Checklist

Before submitting, ensure your manuscript includes: a clear title, abstract, keywords, a structured body, complete references, and required statements (conflict of interest, ethics approvals if applicable, and funding disclosure where relevant). Remove identifying author information from the manuscript file if the journal requires a separate author details sheet for double-blind review.

How to Submit

Email your submission to info.kjmr@kluniversity.in with: (i) manuscript file (PDF/DOCX), (ii) cover message including manuscript title, manuscript type, corresponding author details, and (iii) a statement confirming originality and exclusive submission to KJMR. For editorial communication, you may also copy edboard.kjmr@kluniversity.in.

What Happens After Submission

After submission, the editorial office performs an initial screening for scope, completeness, and basic compliance. Suitable manuscripts proceed to double-blind peer review. Authors are informed of editorial decisions and revision requirements through official email communication.
