Journal Overview

KLEF Journal of Multidisciplinary Research (KJMR) publishes peer-reviewed, Open Access scholarly works that contribute to multidisciplinary knowledge and foster cross-disciplinary collaboration. The journal supports research that is methodologically sound, ethically compliant, and written with clarity and strong scholarly value.

KJMR welcomes original research articles, review articles, short communications, and interdisciplinary studies. Articles are selected through double-blind peer review and editorial quality checks to ensure relevance, rigor, and contribution to the academic community.

Publishing Model

KJMR operates as an Open Access journal. All published articles are freely available to readers without subscription. The journal follows a double-blind peer review policy and publishes two issues per year.

Who Can Submit?

Researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and practitioners are welcome to submit manuscripts. Submissions should be original, unpublished, and not under review elsewhere.
