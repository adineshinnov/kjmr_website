Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how KLEF Journal of Multidisciplinary Research (KJMR) collects, uses, and protects information provided by authors, reviewers, and visitors through the journal website and editorial communication. KJMR is committed to safeguarding privacy and using information only for legitimate journal operations.

This policy may be updated periodically. Users are encouraged to review this page to remain informed about any changes.

Information We May Collect

  • Name, affiliation, and professional contact details
  • Email address and correspondence records
  • Manuscript-related details provided during submission and review
  • Website usage information for performance and security monitoring

How We Use the Information

  • To manage manuscript submissions, peer review, and editorial decisions
  • To communicate with authors and reviewers regarding journal operations
  • To maintain publication records and archive information
  • To improve website functionality and ensure platform security

Confidentiality and Review Process

KJMR follows a double-blind peer review process where reviewer and author identities are protected as per journal workflow. Access to submission data is limited to authorized editorial staff and necessary administrative personnel.

Cookies

Cookies may be used to enhance website functionality and analyze basic traffic patterns. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site features.

Sharing of Information

KJMR does not sell or rent personal information. Information may be shared only when required for legitimate editorial processing or when legally required by applicable regulations.
