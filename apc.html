Article Processing Charge

KLEF Journal of Multidisciplinary Research (KJMR) follows a no-fee policy for authors for standard editorial processing and publication. The journal is published by Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation (KLEF) and is committed to providing Open Access dissemination without imposing mandatory processing or publication charges on authors.

What This Means for Authors

Authors can submit manuscripts, undergo peer review, and publish accepted articles without mandatory submission or publication fees. The journal aims to promote high-quality research dissemination while maintaining strong editorial and ethical standards.

Optional Services

If any optional services are offered in the future (such as additional copyediting requests, printed copies, or special distribution services), they will be clearly communicated and will never influence editorial decisions. Editorial decisions are strictly based on quality, originality, and relevance.
