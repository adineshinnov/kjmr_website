Open Access Publishing

KJMR follows an Open Access publishing model to ensure published research is freely accessible to readers worldwide. The journal is published by Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation (KLEF) and maintains a rigorous double-blind peer review process and professional editorial handling.

APC Policy

KJMR does not charge any mandatory Article Processing Charge (APC) for standard manuscript submission, peer review, editorial handling, and publication. This policy supports wider participation from researchers and promotes accessible scholarly communication.

What’s Included

  • Double-blind peer review by qualified reviewers
  • Editorial screening and manuscript processing
  • Publication formatting and issue-wise archiving
  • Metadata publishing to support discoverability and indexing readiness
  • Open access availability of published content
  • Author communication and publication support
