Privacy Policy

KJMR respects the privacy of authors, reviewers, and readers. Any personal information collected for editorial communication and manuscript processing is used only for journal-related purposes. We do not sell or share personal data for commercial purposes.

Information We May Collect

We may collect basic information such as names, affiliations, email addresses, and manuscript-related details provided during submission or editorial communication. Reviewer identities are protected under the double-blind review process.

Use of Information

Information is used to manage submissions, communicate editorial decisions, coordinate peer review, and maintain publication records. Access is limited to authorized editorial staff and necessary administrative personnel.
