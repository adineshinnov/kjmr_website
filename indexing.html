Current Discovery & Metadata Support

KJMR maintains journal pages with clear scope, editorial board, author guidelines, ethics statements, and contact information. Article-level metadata will be published consistently (title, authors, affiliations, abstract, keywords, publication dates, and citations) together with stable archive pages for each issue.

Planned and Target Platforms

KJMR plans to progressively expand its indexing footprint based on eligibility criteria and publication history. Targeted discovery and indexing services include (subject to acceptance and compliance requirements): Google Scholar, Crossref DOI registration, and additional indexing databases as the journal completes its early publication cycles.

Technical Compliance (Website & Archives)

To support indexing readiness, KJMR follows best practices such as stable navigation, clear archives, accessible PDFs, consistent citation information, and a clearly defined publishing workflow. The journal website is maintained to keep policies up-to-date and ensure accurate information for authors, reviewers, and readers.
