How to Propose a Special Issue

A special issue proposal should include: (i) proposed title and scope, (ii) rationale and relevance, (iii) proposed guest editors with brief profiles, (iv) tentative timeline, and (v) expected themes/topics. Proposals are reviewed by the editorial board for quality, fit, and feasibility.

Submit proposals to: edboard.kjmr@kluniversity.in
