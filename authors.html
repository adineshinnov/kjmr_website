Instructions for Authors

KJMR welcomes original and rigorous multidisciplinary manuscripts. Submissions must be unpublished, not under review elsewhere, and must comply with ethical standards, authorship transparency, and proper citation practices. Manuscripts are evaluated using double-blind peer review.

Types of Manuscripts

KJMR considers Original Research Articles, Review Articles, Short Communications, Case Studies, and Interdisciplinary Studies. All manuscripts must present clear objectives, relevant background, strong methodology, and well-supported conclusions.

Manuscript Structure

A typical manuscript should include: Title, Author Details (submitted separately for double-blind review), Abstract, Keywords, Introduction, Methodology/Materials, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, Acknowledgements (if applicable), Conflict of Interest, Funding Statement (if applicable), References, and Appendices (if needed).

Formatting and Language

Manuscripts must be written in clear academic English with consistent headings and figure/table captions. Figures and tables must be numbered and cited in the text. References must be complete and consistently formatted. Authors are responsible for ensuring originality and plagiarism-free content.

Ethics, Originality, and Plagiarism

Authors must ensure that the submission is original and properly cites all sources. Any form of plagiarism, data fabrication, image manipulation, duplicate submission, or unethical research practice may result in rejection or retraction. For studies involving humans/animals, appropriate approvals and consent statements must be included.

How to Submit

Please submit your manuscript according to the instructions on the Submit page. Authors should include a cover message with: manuscript title, corresponding author details, manuscript type, and a statement confirming originality and exclusive submission to KJMR.
