KLEF Journal of Multidisciplinary Research

KJMR • ISSN: (To be assigned)

KLEF Journal of Multidisciplinary Research (KJMR) is dedicated to publishing high-quality, original, and impactful research that advances knowledge across multiple disciplines and promotes meaningful academic and societal outcomes. The journal provides a scholarly platform for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policy makers to communicate rigorously validated findings, novel methodologies, practical implementations, and evidence-based insights.

KJMR welcomes a wide range of submissions including original research articles, comprehensive review papers, short communications, case studies, and interdisciplinary studies. Manuscripts may span Healthcare, Pharmaceutical Sciences, Agriculture, Management, Law, Science, Engineering, and allied disciplines. Submissions that connect two or more domains are especially encouraged, provided they demonstrate clear problem definition, methodological rigor, appropriate experimentation or analysis, ethical compliance, and well-supported conclusions.

Through a transparent double-blind peer review process and an Open Access publishing model, KJMR aims to ensure timely dissemination of high-quality research and to strengthen cross-disciplinary collaboration by bringing together diverse perspectives, techniques, and applications under a single publication forum.
