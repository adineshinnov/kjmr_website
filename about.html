About the Journal

KJMR ISSN: (To be assigned)

KLEF Journal of Multidisciplinary Research (KJMR) is a peer-reviewed, Open Access journal published by Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation (KLEF), KL University, Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, India. KJMR provides a trusted platform for publishing original and rigorous multidisciplinary research across Healthcare, Pharmaceutical Sciences, Agriculture, Management, Law, Science, Engineering, and allied disciplines. The journal encourages submissions that connect multiple domains and present well-supported insights, methods, or results with clear academic value and real-world relevance.

KJMR is committed to maintaining high editorial standards through a transparent double-blind peer review process. The journal welcomes contributions from researchers, academicians, professionals, and practitioners worldwide. Manuscripts are evaluated based on originality, clarity, rigor, ethical compliance, and significance to the scholarly community.

Aims and Objectives

KJMR aims to publish quality multidisciplinary research that strengthens academic knowledge and supports practical advancement. The journal encourages scholarly work that demonstrates methodological rigor, ethical compliance, meaningful interpretation, and relevance to real-world challenges.

Scope

The journal welcomes manuscripts across (but not limited to) Healthcare, Pharmaceutical Sciences, Agriculture, Management, Law, Science, Engineering, Social Sciences, and allied domains. Interdisciplinary submissions that connect multiple areas and present clear contributions are encouraged.
