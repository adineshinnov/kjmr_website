Cancellations & Refunds

KJMR follows a no-fee policy for standard submission and publication. Therefore, cancellations and refunds related to mandatory publication fees generally do not apply.

Author Withdrawal

Authors may request withdrawal of a manuscript before final acceptance by contacting the editorial office. If a manuscript is under peer review, the author should provide a clear reason for withdrawal. Withdrawal requests are handled through the editorial board.

Contact: edboard.kjmr@kluniversity.in

Future Optional Services

If any optional paid services are introduced in the future, clear service-specific cancellation and refund conditions will be displayed on the relevant page and communicated transparently.
